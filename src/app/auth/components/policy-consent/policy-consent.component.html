Produgie was created to help equip leaders and teams with the mindsets, capabilities and culture required for business performance and growth. Please take a moment to learn more about the information we gather and how it is managed. We need your agreement before we can proceed.


Your Rights as a Produgie User


We are committed to respecting & protecting your rights as a Produgie User and will always be transparent about this with you. To us this means:


  • You have the right to know what information is gathered about you, how this is used, and who (if anyone other than you) can view your information.
  • You have the right to NOT complete any assessment in Produgie, although this action will reduce the accuracy of recommendations Produgie provides you. If your employer has asked you to complete an assessment and you have any questions about this, please speak with them directly.
  • We will always protect your data privacy in line with local and international data privacy & protection laws such as PDPA, GDPR and CCPA. You can review our data policies here.
  • If you leave Produgie, and wish to have your data removed from our data storage, you may do so by informing us of this by email to produgie.support@produgie.com
  • We will safely store & manage your data in a location that is compliant with local data laws in which the data originates.
  • If we have experienced a breach in our security in a way that might compromise your data, we will let you know within 72 hours.
  • When we change our data retention or storage policies or practices, we will let you know and ask for your consent within 1 month of making these changes.
  • If you change employers, you may take over your own license as long as you let us know before your license expires. We may charge a small fee for this service.
  • If you leave your current employer and go to another Produgie customer, we will migrate your account if your employer wishes to pay for your license and a small transfer fee. All our policies are published on our website and please review these any time at: www.produgie.com/terms

Viewing Rights


In Produgie, you can always know who can view your information. If your company has configured Produgie so that a person has viewing rights over any of your information, you will see this in your Viewing Rights panel. After you agree to the Produgie User Rights, you can view or edit your viewing rights by clicking on Settings > Viewing Rights.
